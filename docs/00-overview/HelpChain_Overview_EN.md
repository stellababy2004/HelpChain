# HelpChain

## Institutional Coordination Platform for Requests and Operational Follow-Up

HelpChain is a digital platform for institutions and coordinated field actors that need to manage incoming requests, assign follow-up responsibility, and maintain traceable operational workflows across structures.

The platform is positioned for France-first institutional use, with emphasis on accountability, role-based access, tenant scoping, and operational visibility rather than marketplace-style matching.

## Core Capabilities

- Intake and management of structured requests
- Operational assignment and follow-up across authorized teams
- Role-based access with traceability and auditability
- Health, reporting, and governance controls for sustained operations

## Target Organisations

- Public institutions
- Territorial and municipal actors
- Associations operating in structured coordination environments
- Cross-organisational response teams working under controlled governance

## Product Characteristics

- Request-centric operating model
- Multi-structure access boundaries
- Administrative auditability
- Production-oriented deployment and runbook coverage
