# HelpChain
## Social & Healthcare Support Platform

**HelpChain** is a digital platform designed to facilitate
connections between people in need and qualified volunteers or professionals.

---

## Objectives

- Centralized management of help requests
- Volunteer coordination
- Security, traceability, and compliance
- Multilingual accessibility

---

## Technical Guarantees

- CI pipeline blocks on any inconsistency
- Automatic database schema verification
- Transactional tests with rollback
- Secure authentication (2FA)

---

## Target Audience

- NGOs
- Local authorities
- Public institutions
- Associations
